Pig-Dice Game

How to play:

  • Player in turn rolls the die repeatedly (click "roll dice" button).
  • The goal is to accumulate as many points as possible by adding up the numbers rolled on the die (displayed in "current" box).
  • If a player rolls a "1", their turn is over and any points they have accumulated during this turn are lost.
  • Rolling a "1" doesn't wipe out the player's entire score from previous turns, just the total earned during that particular roll.
  • Each player's score is displayed under the player's title.
  • A player can also choose to hold (click "hold" button) if they do not want to take a chance of rolling a "1" and losing all the points from their current turn. If the player chooses to hold, all of the points rolled during that turn are added to their score.
  • The first player to reach a total of 100 or more points, wins the game.
